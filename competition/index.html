Summer Competition This page was last updated May 24, 2022

We are holding a summer workout competition! Here is how it works: you do a workout (can be anything), use our conversions to calculate how many points you scored, and then log them in the Google Sheet. Whomever scores the most points by the start of school wins!

Source code on GitHub. Open for pull requests.
